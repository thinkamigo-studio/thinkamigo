Six distinct brand elements. Each does a specific job and carries its own visual register. Together they describe the whole practice. Select any element to read more.

Studio thinkamigo

The practice. The atelier umbrella.

▼
The umbrella

The practice in full. Forty years of creative and production practice. Named in the register of Studio International - serious, authoritative, European. Not a brand name chosen for effect; a designation that places the work in a tradition of independent studio practice.

Studio thinkamigo is the entity that encompasses everything below. The commercial vehicle is Thinkamigo Limited, incorporated February 2012.

Visual register: Authoritative and considered. Not nostalgic - the past informs but does not decorate.

methodology

thinkOS

The methodology. The connective tissue.

▼
Connective tissue

The operating system of the practice. thinkOS is not only a working methodology - it is a cultural position formed over six decades. A methodology can be copied. A cultural position cannot.

The tools, the bundles, the workflows are expressions of a coherent world view that took a lifetime to assemble. Conceptual models, Six Sombreros, context bundles, editorial workflow, framework architecture.

A thinkINTELLIGENCE client is buying access to someone running thinkOS. thinkamigoWORKSHOP produces the artefacts. thinkamigoEDITIONS and thinkamigoSTREAMS carry them into the world. thinkOS underpins and enables all of it.

Visual register: Conceptual, diagrammatic. Clean systems thinking. The intelligence made visible.

thinkINTELLIGENCE

The premium service.

▼
think iS.

The premium concierge service. Full name: think Intelligence Service. Visual mark: think iS. Pronounced: think eye ess.

Three tiers: personal concierge (referral and invitation only, Paul's direct time and expertise); thinkIS SME consultancy (scalable, pre-configured methodology); thinkIS Learn (self-serve online course).

A thinkINTELLIGENCE client is not buying AI tools. They are buying access to a practitioner who knows how to use them - and the cultural formation that makes that use distinctive.

Visual register: Azure blue and gold. Paris, South of France, the Riviera. Roger Moore's The Saint - debonair, witty, internationally mobile. Intelligence worn lightly.

thinkamigoWORKSHOP

The making place.

▼
Production

Where things are made. Websites, video, illustration, booklets, exhibition panels, sound recordings, storyboards, wearables. Digital production as craft. The Bauhaus workshop model applied to digital making.

The WORKSHOP produces the artefacts that enter the world through thinkamigoEDITIONS and thinkamigoSTREAMS.

This Amigos space is part of thinkamigoWORKSHOP - tools and resources for the production process.

Visual register: Digital, precise, dark mode, code aesthetic. VS Code, CSS, JSON, terminal as workbench.

thinkamigoEDITIONS

The permanent record.

▼
Publisher

Registered publisher. ISBN management, British Library legal deposit, NTU collaborations. Things that will exist in 100 years.

Eleven publications to date spanning exhibition catalogues, heritage guides, anthologies and research publications. Two forthcoming in 2026: Coaltime Days and A to Z of Coal Mining.

Visual register: Warm, editorial, print-informed. The weight and considered quality of a well-designed publication.

thinkamigoSTREAMS

The living presence.

▼
Channels

Transient digital channels. Substack, Instagram, LinkedIn, Pinterest. Real-time audience engagement.

The STREAMS build the audience for the EDITIONS. EDITIONS is accretion - the permanent deposit. STREAMS is efflux - content in motion. Both are needed.

Visual register: Fluid, dynamic, social-native. In motion rather than fixed.

thinkOS is the connective tissue. A thinkINTELLIGENCE client is buying access to someone running thinkOS. thinkamigoWORKSHOP produces the artefacts that enter the world through thinkamigoEDITIONS and thinkamigoSTREAMS. The methodology underpins and enables everything else.
